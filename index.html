There are only 6,800 Eastern Lowland Gorillas left...

The population is estimated to have fallen from 17,000 in the 1990s to 6,800 currently. These gorillas only live in the Democratic Republic of the Congo. They are threatened by deforestation combined with a lack of legal protection for their habitats.
